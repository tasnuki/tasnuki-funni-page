repository of stuff

These addons are for Garry's Mod, you must install them locally.

These mods are very simple and were made for friends. Keep that in mind.

Nemesis

Configurable silly mod that spawns a npc of your choosing randomly.

You can set it's class, weapon, health, and min and max interval of the spawn timer.

Convar prefix is nemesis_

I recommend setting some tough npcs, but you can use it for whatever.

Avoid using in underwater maps, it has a check to see if nav is underwater and if it is it will try again - this may lead to overflow lol. Feel free to remove that check yourself.

Thief Footsteps

Simple sound replacement mod that replaces footsteps to something akin to Thief games.

BETA MALE

Beta royale playermodels and DRGBase are required for this addon!!!

Simple bot that just runs around and kills players and npcs and stuff.

It has 3 weapons it can use: crowbar, shotgun, revolver

It can hear voice chat and there are cvars related to it, check them out.

Funny ass ragdoll physics sfx

Simple sound replacement that just replaces ragdoll impact sounds with those from Gasmask Citizen 22

Healthkit spawner

Server script that spawns healthkits and healthvials around the map.

Uses navmesh for spawns, so if there is no navmesh it may throw an error.

Script is controlled by using chat, the commands are admin-only:

!itemcount number = how many to spawn, !itemstock = spawn, !itemclear = clear spawned items.

You can edit the lua file and add your own items if you wish. I think the chance stuff is kinda jank though so watch out for that.

Epic Father Grigori death scream

This mod was made by my friend.

Makes it so when Father Grigori dies he plays his death scream, it has insane range and pitch is randomized.

Greghunt squad playermodels

4 playermodels for a gamemode that is currenly in limbo.

Greghunt greggy

Requires DRGbase!!!!

Various verions of greg bots.

He's basically like a little boss fight, shit gets real as soon as you spawn him in; he spawns in a random location.

He can do things like place traps, teleport around, go sneaky beaky mode, create fog.

The healthbar thing is a bit janky, but you can turn it off by typing !greghp 0.

Fun to mess around with friends.

Snuki Repairs BETA (real)

Collection of old Snuki Repairs builds.

Contains 7 builds, all totalling to 1,81 GB.

The builds are highly jank! Earlier builds especially didn't even have item scrolling, you have to bind the buttons manually! (ew!)

If you're subscribed to the released version of Snuki Repairs that is on steam you should unsub before installing any version.

You must have DRGbase and Pixelation Shader installed and enabled. Find them on the workshop.

Also warning: the entire map is in a cursed language, consult with how2game .txt to find your way!! AND it also may contain CRINGE!!!!!! (hell.)
